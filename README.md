# 🏙️ The Montgomery Pulse
**Predictive Urban Vitality & Commercialization AI**

> An autonomous, predictive, and prescriptive Hybrid AI engine that simulates the economic future of a city block before a single brick is laid. Built for the World Wide Vibes Hackathon 2026.

---

## 💡 The Inspiration
City planning and commercial real estate development are fundamentally broken. When cities try to revitalize vacant lots—or developers underwrite a new retail space—they rely on static, backward-looking heat maps. It’s a high-risk guessing game that often leads to commercial deserts or gentrification friction. I wanted to build a tool that replaces hope with deterministic math and generative strategy.

## 🚀 Core Capabilities
The Montgomery Pulse is a closed-loop **B2G Commercialization Platform** that de-risks urban development through three main engines:

* **Predictive Simulation (Agent-Based Modeling):** Spawns thousands of autonomous consumer agents weighted by Montgomery’s daily population flows. Agents make localized spending decisions penalized by a 'City Friction Index' (calculated from 911 dispatch data) and rewarded by a 'Live Vibe Score' (real-time neighborhood sentiment).
* **Prescriptive Engine:** Mathematically scans the trade radius of any city surplus property, identifies commercial gaps, automatically recommends the exact zoning type needed, and projects 12 months of revenue.
* **Hybrid AI Strategy Boardroom:** Runs a deterministic SciPy optimization algorithm to find the exact consumer parameters needed to maximize market capture, then feeds that math into a Generative AI "Chief Strategy Officer" to translate the numbers into a human-readable operational and marketing strategy.

## 🧠 The Hybrid AI Architecture
This is a true Hybrid AI system that distinctly separates mathematical optimization from generative reasoning:
* **Frontend:** Python, Streamlit, and PyDeck (for real-time geospatial visualization).
* **Data Ingestion:** Merges four distinct Montgomery Open Data CSVs alongside the official city surplus property list.
* **Live Sentiment:** Integrates **Bright Data's Web Scraper API** to dynamically extract a live "Vibe Score" based on current web sentiment.
* **Deterministic Math:** Utilizes **SciPy (L-BFGS-B optimization)** to evaluate the stochastic Monte Carlo simulation and deterministically solve for the global maximum market capture rate.
* **Generative Strategy:** Integrates **Google's Gemini 1.5 Flash API** to ingest the complex mathematical outputs and generate executive-level business strategies.
